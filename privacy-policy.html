Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using Mjakazi Connect.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mjakazi Connect ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our website and related services (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and contact details when you register or contact us.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, actions taken, and device/technical data collected via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Profile data for caregivers or families necessary to provide matching and discovery functionality.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Facilitate caregiver discovery and communication.
  • Personalize content and measure site performance.
  • Ensure trust, safety, and compliance with applicable laws.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Service, or when necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You may access, update, or delete certain information by contacting us. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the Service. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@mjakaziconnect.com.
